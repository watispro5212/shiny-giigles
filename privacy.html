Privacy Protocol

ESTABLISHED // MARCH 2026


01. Data Ingestion

Nexus Protocol operates on a principle of least-privilege. We scan and store only essential metadata required for your node operation: User IDs, Guild IDs, and permission bitfields. We do not intercept unauthorized cleartext transmissions unless specified by a Node Administrator (Auto-Mod settings).

02. Asset Security

Credit reserves, inventory items, and neural rank data are stored in isolated database clusters. This information remains within the Nexus network and is never offloaded to third-party data harvesters.

03. Oblivion Rights

Any Operative may request full data severance. Removing the bot from your node suspends active processing, but internal balances may persist for future reintegration. Contact Support for absolute erasure.

04. Security Standards

We utilize advanced encryption for all uplink packets. However, the digital frontier is never fully secure. Users engage with Nexus at their own risk within the wider inter-node network.
