How Sharding Works

Discord enforces physical limits on the number of servers a single WebSocket connection can interact with. Once Nexus reached 2,500 guilds, we initiated the DJS ShardingManager protocol across our cluster infrastructure.

Each shard acts as a completely independent process with its own chunk of servers, communicating back to the IPC (Inter-Process Communication) master index. Removing the web-socket proxy overhead has secured 100% stable uptime, as each shard handles localized traffic automatically.
